About the School

The name of the school

The official name of the school is JUABOSO SENIOR HIGH SCHOOL, often shortened as "JUASEC".


The school is one of the Senior High Schools established in 1991 as part of the 1987 education reforms. It started as Day/Hostel with a student's population of one hundred and thirty (130).
It had Mr. J. K. Ahenkorah as the first headmaster. The school collapsed in 1996 but was re-established in 1997, thus time with Mr. J. K. Taylor as the headmaster. The school continued as
Day and Hostel when it was re-established in 1997 until 4th November 2013 when it obtained a Boarding status.

The school started with three (3) programmes namely Science, Agriculture, and Business with Home Economics and General Arts being added on in 2002, 2004 respectively.
The first Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (SSCE) for the school was written in the year 2000.
