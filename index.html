Boarding House

The school runs a boarding system with facilities for both boys and girls. There are four houses for male and four for female students. The houses are:

House 1 aka AHWOI HOUSE House 2 aka NANA NKRUMAH HOUSE House 3 aka E. K. MENSAH HOUSE House 4 aka YAA AMINA HOUSE

Each student, whether a day or boarding student, belong to one house and stay there for the duration of their time at the school. No student is permitted to stay in a different home or switch residences for any reason.


Sports and Games

A healthy mind can only exist in a healthy body, and it is said that "all work and no play makes jack a dull boy." The school prioritises sports and other social activities in accordance with these adages. The school takes part in all organised sporting events at the zonal and regional levels.

In order to teach students the values of fair play, good sportsmanship, and how to accept defeat with grace and display magnanimity in victory, the school encourages students to participate in sports and games.

About

The school was one of the senior secondary schools set up in 1991 as part of the education reforms that began in 1987. It began as a day/hostel with 130 students. Mr. J. K. Ahenkorah was the school's first headmaster. Under Mr. J. K. Taylor's leadership, the school reopened in 1997 after closing in 1996. When the school was reopened in 1997, it operated as a Day and Hostel until it was granted Boarding status on November 4, 2013. The first Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (SSCE) for the school was written in the year 2000.

The School Team

June 4, 2023 - The students of Juaboso Senior High School (SHS) showcased their exceptional athletic prowess at the recently concluded zonal athletic and sporting competitions held at Dadieso SHS.With their remarkable skills and determination, the Juaboso SHS students emerged as true champions, leaving an indelible mark on the event.

The competition, which brought together talented athletes from various schools in the region, was a highly anticipated event. Spectators filled the stadium with excitement and anticipation, eager to witness the fierce battles between the participating schools.

The Juaboso SHS students did not disappoint, as they demonstrated their commitment to excellence in various sporting disciplines.The track events witnessed a dominant display by the Juaboso SHS athletes. The school's relay team, consisting of four exceptionally talented runners, blazed past their competitors to claim the gold medal in both the boys' and girls' categories. Their exceptional teamwork and lightning-fast speed left spectators in awe.

The outstanding achievements of the Juaboso SHS students were met with jubilation and applause from their fellow students, teachers, and parents. The Headmaster of Juaboso SHS, Mr. Christian Dedjoe Djokotoe, expressed his immense pride in the students' exceptional performances. He commended their dedication, hard work, and disciplined approach, which he believes were instrumental in their success.
